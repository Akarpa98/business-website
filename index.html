About

I created Karpa Structure to help people build stable lives across borders, especially those navigating migration with limited resources or under difficult circumstances. Living internationally has shaped my entire life. I have personally navigated multiple immigration systems, residencies, and administrative frameworks across countries. Professionally, I have worked in legal, regulatory, and policy roles across Europe, supporting EU institutions, private-sector clients, and international organisations. This includes work across all 27 EU Member States, the UK, Ukraine, and Namibia. In parallel, I have operated as a self-employed professional in multiple jurisdictions, giving me direct experience in cross-border freelance work, tax structuring, and administrative setup.
