Services Overview

Services are tailored on a case-by-case basis following an initial consultation. During this consultation, I assess each client’s individual circumstances, objectives, financial constraints, and preferred destinations.

  • Residency and migration pathways
  • Bureaucracy navigation
  • Administrative setup abroad (tax, social security, health insurance, legal residency)
  • Tax and freelancer structuring
  • Affordable country comparisons
  • Step-by-step relocation planning

Depending on the client’s needs, services may be provided as a one-off consultation, a structured relocation roadmap, or ongoing support.

This service is designed for:

  • Individuals relocating with limited financial resources
  • Remote workers and freelancers
  • Individuals leaving unstable environments
  • People needing help navigating bureaucracy across borders

Note: Fees are determined after consultation based on complexity.
